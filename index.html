THE ORIGIN It was a cold winter day with a chilling breeze. Arya the Oreo was standing on Mount. Triangle. She was waiting for her newly made friend who has a weird addiction to bananas. Suddenly, she heard a voice in the distance, “Hey Arya, your name reminds me of Oreos.” the voice said. “TISSHHUUUEEEE!” Arya cried. “Can I bring a friend?” Ishu the tissue asked, not caring about Arya’s scream. “I don’t know, why not?” Arya replied. Suddenly, a girl who had two giant snakes on her head loved snails, and looked like Wednesday said, “HI,” she said in a weird menacing voice. Lightning struck the mountain. Suddenly Arya said, “Do you guys like- like anime???????????????????” Suddenly Ishanvi turned into a weeb. (someone who is very VERY addicted to anime.)
